# Uptime — Product Overview

## Product Identity
- **Name**: Uptime
- **Tagline**: Never miss a class. Stay ahead. Show up.
- **Category**: Student Productivity / Digital Timetable & Academic Time Management App
- **Platform**: Web application (mobile-first, progressive design)

## Target Audience
- **Primary Users**: Students at tertiary institutions — universities, colleges, and polytechnics
- **Persona**: The busy undergraduate or postgraduate student juggling multiple courses, assignments, tests, and exams
- **Pain Points**:
  - Forgetting about scheduled classes entirely
  - Arriving late to lectures without sufficient advance warning
  - No structured system for preparing for tests and exams
  - Starting the academic day without direction or motivation

## Product Vision
Uptime is a creative digital timetable designed exclusively for tertiary-level students. It combines intelligent class scheduling with proactive alarm reminders, structured study tools, and daily motivational nudges — all in one beautifully designed app. Uptime's mission is to make every student punctual, prepared, and inspired, every single day.

## Core Features

### 1. Timetable Setup
Students can create and manage their complete weekly class schedule — course names, lecture times, venues, and days — in a clean, intuitive calendar interface. The timetable is the student's central scheduling hub.

### 2. Smart Class Alarm Reminders (Ringtone Integration)
Uptime fires ringtone-backed alarm reminders **1 hour** and **30 minutes** before each scheduled class. Students receive two-stage advance warnings with enough time to prepare and travel, eliminating lateness and forgotten lectures entirely.

### 3. Study Timers for Tests & Exams
Dedicated countdown and structured study timers help students organise their preparation sessions for upcoming tests and exams. Students set a study goal, start the timer, and stay locked in for the full session.

### 4. Word of the Day — Daily Motivational Pop-Up
Every time a student opens the app, a curated motivational quote or "Word of the Day" message greets them with encouragement. This daily positive nudge sets an inspiring tone before every class, study session, or exam.

## Brand & Tone
- **Brand Name**: Uptime
- **Brand Voice**: Empowering, modern, youthful, and supportive
- **Design Philosophy**: Vibrant yet clean; premium feel tailored to student culture
- **Primary Color**: Deep indigo (#4338ca) — focused, intelligent, trustworthy
- **Accent Color**: Cyan (#06b6d4) — energetic and fresh
- **Neutral Base**: Slate-on-white — clean, readable, modern
- **Personality**: Like a smart campus companion — practical, encouraging, and always on time

## Strategic Principles
1. **Punctuality First** — Every feature is engineered to help students be where they need to be, on time.
2. **Motivation by Default** — The app opens with encouragement, not a to-do list.
3. **Simplicity Over Complexity** — Students should set a reminder in under a minute, not navigate complexity.
4. **Study Discipline** — Structured timers embed repeatable academic preparation habits.

## Scope & Constraints
- Web application with mobile-first, responsive design
- Core features accessible without a login barrier
- Designed for accessibility, speed, and clarity across all device sizes
- Audience-exclusive: purpose-built for tertiary student workflows only

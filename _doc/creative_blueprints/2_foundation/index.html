The Problem

Tertiary students are losing grades — and time — to poor scheduling habits.

Forgotten Classes

No tracking system means students only discover they had a lecture after it already ended.

Chronic Lateness

Without timely warnings, students rush and still arrive late — disrupting focus and standing.

Unstructured Prep & Lost Motivation

Tests and exams catch students off guard. No study system and no daily drive lead straight to burnout.

The Solution

Uptime gives every student a system — and the motivation — to show up and succeed.

Smart Timetable + Dual Alarms

Set up your full class schedule once. Ringtone reminders fire automatically 1 hour and 30 minutes before every lecture.

Focused Study Timers

Countdown timers built for tests and exams embed structured preparation habits automatically.

Daily Word of the Day

Every app open greets students with a curated motivational message — starting each day with purpose and confidence.
